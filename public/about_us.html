About Us

MealLink is an innovative platform dedicated to minimizing food wastage while helping those in need. Our mission is to bridge the gap between those who have surplus food and orphanages, NGOs, and communities that are in need of it.

Our Mission

At MealLink, we believe that no food should go to waste while people go hungry. We empower individuals and organizations to donate excess food effortlessly, making a positive impact on the lives of those less fortunate.

How It Works

  • Donate Food: Anyone with surplus food can easily fill out a simple donation form.
  • Find Donations: Orphanages and NGOs can browse nearby donations based on location.
  • Connect and Share: Seamless communication between donors and recipients to ensure successful food distribution.

Why Choose MealLink?

- Easy to Use: A modern, user-friendly interface that makes the process hassle-free.
- Community Impact: Your donations directly support local communities and those in need.
- Real-Time Availability: View nearby donations in real time, ensuring quick and efficient distribution.
